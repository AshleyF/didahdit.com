This is messy prototype still in development.

It uses the USB paddle interface from Ham Radio Solutions ("Store" tab).

It's a keyer that can be used for practice with a nifty visualization of paddle state (currently doesn't support straight keys).

It will also connect to a radio over USB/Bluetooth and will act as a proper keyer.

Usage tips:

  • Works with this USB paddle interface
  • Or press left/right CTRL or square brackets to send.
  • Press BACKSPACE to clear.
  • With Pileup on, send CQ
    • Operators will reply with callsigns
    • Send partial calls followed by ? to narrow (or just ? or AGN to repeat)
    • Complete call followed by ? will reply with RR [call]
    • Send [call] and signal report, they will reply in kind and include U.S. state
    • Finsh with a thank you and a dit-dit and they will too and new ops will jump in
